ABOUT US

Innovation Consortium or ICONS South Luzon is a regional startup ecosystem consortium that endeavors
in partnership with various industry players, line agencies, and other agencies in sponsoring
innovation events that support startup development and spur innovation.

OBJECTIVES

The program of ICONS South Luzon establishes a regional startup development plan, develop strategies to promote the startup programs of PCIEERD/DOST, establish a pool of mentors and conduct mentorship programs for startups to increase fundability, craft a skills enhancement program for the startup enablers, and implement collaborative programs/activities for the ecosystem.

TARGET ACTIVITIES
Organize a Regional Startup Ecosystem Consortium
Develop Promotion Strategies for Various Startup Programs
Create, Establish, and Implement a Regional Startup Development Program
Establish a Mentor Pool and Regional Startup Mentorship Program
Implement a Skill Enhancement Program for TBIs and Other Startup Enablers
Implement Collaborative Programs/Activities for the Ecosystem
